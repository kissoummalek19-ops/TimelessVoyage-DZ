What is TimelessVoyage DZ?

It’s a culture-tech platform that links Algeria’s heritage sites and artifacts to a digital twin. Visitors scan a QR code to open a curated experience matched to each site: images with layered facts, HD videos, or AR/VR reconstructions.

  • Time-rings UI showing civilizations: Stone Age, Phoenicians, Numidians, Romans, Vandals, Byzantines, Islamic & Andalusian, Ottoman, Modern era.
  • Guided narration in multiple languages with AI voice.
  • Built-in marketplace for authentic crafts.
